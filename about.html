About FarmBuddy

Empowering farmers with data-driven insights for better decisions

Who We Are

FarmBuddy is a cutting-edge platform designed to help farmers leverage NASA Earth observation data, AI, and advanced technologies to improve productivity, sustainability, and economic growth. Our platform offers features like Predictive Climate Analytics, Soil Health Assessment, AI-based Disease Detection, and more to make farming smarter and more efficient.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance agricultural productivity by providing farmers with the tools they need to make informed decisions using real-time data. We believe that by reducing uncertainties in farming and promoting sustainable practices, we can make a positive impact on both the environment and the global food supply chain.
